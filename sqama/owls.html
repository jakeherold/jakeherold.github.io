How you can give a rescued owl a new lease on life!

Ever since the worldwide poplularity of the Harry Potter films, owls have seen an explosion of popularity as pets. Of the tens of thousands of children have begged their parents for one of these adorable birds, many have quickly learned that, just like any pet, owls require a considerable investment of time, love, and care.

It doesn't help that their most favorite choice of food was next years most popular christmas pet gift made famous by the Disney/Pixar movie, Ratatouille.
