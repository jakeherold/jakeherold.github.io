About Us

History:

Founded in 1814, SQAMA was created to take in and adopt out unusual and exotic animals, specifically those made popular by ludicrious children's movies. Originally a reaction to the smash hit, "Hey Kids! Racoons Make Fun Pets!" SQAMA has been on the forefront of finding new homes for now-unwanted animals.

Mission:

Rescue and re-home animals that people bought in rash decisions.
